Mint & Raspberry Macaroons

Show stopping and eye catching, this funky and fresh dessert is sure to be the life of any party!

Prep Time:

2 hours

Bake Time:

40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 100g egg whites
  • 100g white sugar
  • 105g almond flour
  • 325g icing sugar
  • 1/2tsp peppermint extract
  • 100g butter
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • 60ml double cream
  • 1tsp raspberry extract
  • 1tsp pink food colouring paste
  • 1tsp teal food colouring paste

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 150˚c/300˚F/Gas mark 2. Line 2 pans with parchment paper.

  2. Sift the almond flour and 125g of icing sugar together and set aside.

  3. Mix the egg whites, white sugar and vanilla extract on low for 3 minutes, then beat on medium speed for 3 minutes and finally on high for 3 minutes. Add the teal gel paste and peppermint extract, beat for one final minute. The meringue should be very stiff.

  4. Add the almond and icing sugar mixture and fold it into the meringue until it has deflated. It should look like molten lava that will hold its shape for a bit and then melt into the mixture.

  5. Pipe the mixture into 24 small circles on the parchment paper. old the pan and firmly smack it against the counter a couple of times to remove the air bubbles.

  6. Bake in the oven for approximately 18 minutes or until the macaroons can be easily removed from the parchment paper. If they stick or the shell comes off the but bottom sticks, bake them a little longer.

  7. While the macaroons are cooling, beat the butter and vanilla extract until creamy.

  8. Add 200g icing sugar slowly, simultaneously beating.

  9. Add in the double cream and beat until light and fluffy.

  10. Mix in the raspberry extract and pink food coloring.

  11. Add the mixture to a piping bag and assembled two halves of the macaroon shells with the raspberry filling. Repeat the process until all the macaroons have been used.
