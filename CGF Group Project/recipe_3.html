Strawberry Spring Cheesecake

Decadently rich and no baking required, this recipe is the perfect dessert to kick back, relax, and let it do all the work!

Prep Time:

20 minutes

Bake Time:

5 minutes plus chilling

Ingredients:

  • 160g digestives
  • 75g butter
  • 35g light brown soft sugar
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 110g caster sugar
  • 100g strawberries
  • 100g cherries
  • 75g raspberries

Method:

  1. Crush the digestive biscuits in a food bag with a rolling pin or in the food processor. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Take off heat and stir in the brown sugar and biscuit crumbs.

  2. Line the base of a 20cm cake tin with baking parchment. Press the biscuit into the bottom of the tin and chill in the fridge while making the topping.

  3. Beat together the mascarpone and caster sugar until smooth and creamy. Spread over the base and chill for a couple of hours.

  4. In a saucepan, mix the cherries and raspberries together with 50ml of water and 1tbsp of caster sugar. Cook for 10-15 minutes or until the mixture has thickened. Allow to cool for 20 minutes before pouring onto the cheesecake, and refrigerate for a further half hour

  5. Once set, top with chopped strawberries. For an extra decadent look, we recommend topping with seasonal flowers
