Blood Orange Tart

Prep Time: Under 30 minutes

Bake Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200g sugar, plus extra for dusting
  • 3 blood oranges, juice and zest
  • 1 tsp orange blossom water
  • 2 eggs, plus 6 yolks
  • 200g butter
  • 25cm sweetened shortcrust pastry tart shell, blind baked (you can use ready-made)
  • 3 blood oranges, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 1 tbsp demerara sugar
  • Double cream or custard, to serve

Method:

Preheat the oven to 180°c/160°c fan/350°f/Gas 4.


Whisk the sugar, orange juice and zest, orange blossom water, eggs and egg yolks together in a bowl until well combined.


Add the butter and set over a pan over simmering water. Make sure not to let the base of the bowl touch the water.


Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring regularly until the butter has melted and the mixture has thickened.


Pour the mixture into the cooked pastry case. Cover with clingfilm to prevent a skin forming, and set aside to cool.


Place the orange slices on the cooled tart and sprinkle over the demerara sugar.


Using a cooking blowtorch, heat the sugar until caramelised.


Serve the sliced tart with double cream or custard.
