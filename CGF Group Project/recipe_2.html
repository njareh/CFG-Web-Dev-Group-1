Blood Orange Tart

Perfect as a refreshing summer dessert, our quick and easy recipe is both a treat for the eyes and stomach!

Prep Time:

Under 30 minutes

Bake Time:

20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200g sugar, plus extra for dusting
  • 3 blood oranges, juice and zest
  • 1 tsp orange blossom water
  • 2 eggs, plus 6 yolks
  • 200g butter
  • 25cm sweetened shortcrust pastry tart shell, blind baked (you can use ready-made)
  • 3 blood oranges, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 1 tbsp demerara sugar
  • Double cream or custard, to serve

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°c/160°c fan/350°f/Gas 4.

  2. Whisk the sugar, orange juice and zest, orange blossom water, eggs and egg yolks together in a bowl until well combined.

  3. Add the butter and set over a pan over simmering water. Make sure not to let the base of the bowl touch the water.

  4. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring regularly until the butter has melted and the mixture has thickened.

  5. Pour the mixture into the cooked pastry case. Cover with clingfilm to prevent a skin forming, and set aside to cool.

  6. Place the orange slices on the cooled tart and sprinkle over the demerara sugar.

  7. Using a cooking blowtorch, heat the sugar until caramelised.

  8. Serve the sliced tart with double cream or custard.
